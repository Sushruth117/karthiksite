The Solar System is made of a bunch of planets orbiting around the Sun. But they are not orbiting on a flat plane.

The planets are also very far from each other, and their sizes are tiny compared to the vastness that is our Solar System.

The rocky planets of the Inner Solar System are orbiting tightly together, while the gas giants are chilling at a comfortable distance from the Sun.
