Karthigeyan R: Remembering a Star Among Us

The Solar System was formed more than 4.5 Billion years ago, our Solar System is a group made of our Sun, a star and other celestial bodies orbiting around it.

Another such star born on Earth was Karthigeyan R.

"This is a top-view model of the Solar System, and as familiar as it seems, it's very different than it really is."

I think this is what Karthik would have pointed out if I had shown this site. This website is a place in the cosmos dedicated in remembrance of Karthik. He left us few years back due to some complications encountered during a trek. But his star always shines bright and this website is dedicated to cherish his memories. And to be a proponent of space sciences which Karthik was so fond of. He was an Astrophysicist after all.
